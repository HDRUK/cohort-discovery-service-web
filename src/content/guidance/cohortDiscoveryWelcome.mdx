Cohort Discovery helps you quickly determine whether cohorts relevant to your research or clinical trial exist, where the data is held, and their size, without needing to contact each organisation individually.
<br/>
You can run real-time queries across multiple pseudonymised datasets, spanning clinical patient records, research-consented participant records, and other data collections. Matching results are returned as numerical counts, allowing you to assess feasibility across numerous datasets at a glance, and then connect directly with the appropriate Data Custodians to take next steps.
