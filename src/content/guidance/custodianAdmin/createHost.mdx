
**A Host** is the credentialed connection to your data source. After you create a Host, the system generates a **Client ID** and **Client Secret**


You will use these credentials when configuring BUNNYs. Specifically, copy the Host's Client ID and Client Secret into the following environment variables:

```dotenv
  TASK_API_USERNAME=<HOST_CLIENT_ID>  
  TASK_API_PASSWORD=<HOST_CLIENT_SECRET>
```

For detailed setup instructions, see [Hutch bunny config](https://hutch.health/bunny/config).


Keep the Client Secret private (do not commit it to source control or share it in screenshots/logs).
